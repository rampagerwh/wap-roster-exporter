WAP Roster Exporter

Usage:

  • Use the "Load roster" button to load a roster.
  • Use the browser's print function (or share/print/save to pdf in mobile) to save/print the list.
  • Toggle units and/or section collapsing by clicking in their header.
  • Drag the â‡• in the headers to reorder units/sections.
  • Use the checkboxes and the "Hide checked elements" toggle to hide/display elements. You can use this functionality to generate a public version of your list without the hidden elements, or just to hide elements in the list you don't want to print.
  • You can resize the comment section's text area to fill in space and avoid splitting units.
  • You can change the colors using the pickers on the right.
  • If you wish to avoid printing the URL of the webpage, time, page number, etc, play with the settings in the printing dialog.
